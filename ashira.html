Origin

Ashira comes from family-run farms on the western slopes of Lake Atitlán — some of the most minerally complex volcanic soil on Earth. The high altitude slows the cherry's development by weeks, concentrating sugars and building acidity that washed processing preserves cleanly. What you taste is the mountain.
