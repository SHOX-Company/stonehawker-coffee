Named for the lake itself, Atitlán is our flagship medium roast — the one we put in front of people who say they don't like specialty coffee. Natural processing lets the cherry's sugars ferment into the bean, adding body and a sweetness that doesn't need cream or sugar to come through. The volcanic mineral profile runs underneath everything, giving it depth that most single-origin coffees never reach.
